The Idea

Solid oak, built to last

Crafted from high-quality solid oak, the gazebo features a classic pitched roof that adds timeless elegance and excellent weather protection.

The trellis panels on the side facing the fence and one side of the front opening will give you lovely privacy while creating a wonderful opportunity for climbing plants like roses, jasmine, or clematis to grow and add colour and fragrance.

It sits perfectly on your stunning new porcelain tiles and will become your favourite spot to:

  • Enjoy morning coffee or evening drinks
  • Relax and read a book
  • Entertain family and friends
  • Simply soak up the garden views in comfort and style

The Plan

A place to sit out, whatever the weather

Solid oak posts and a full gable roof give it real shelter, with lattice trellis panels along one side facing the fence and part of the front opening for privacy without closing it in. It sits beautifully on your new porcelain patio.
